Accuracy Accounting Timeline

Scroll below to learn more about the history behind our company!

April 2009

The Meeting of a Team

Daniel, David, and Tim first started working together at an accounting firm in Seattle and quickly hit it off.

June 2015

The Birth of an Idea

The three of them decided that they were tired of working for other people. Soon, they were dreaming of ways that they could work for themselves one day. Chief among them: start their own firm.

February 2016

Founding of a Firm

Finally, after much deliberation, the three of them quit their jobs and decided to take the next step in creating their own accounting firm. Thus, Accuracy Accounting was created.

January 2017

The Move

After being located in their Seattle apartments for the first year, they decided that they were confident enough to take the next step by renting out an office space.

June 2017

The Growth

After successfully growing, they decided to do the next big thing: hire some employees. Here, Steve and Abby joined the team as the first two full-time associates.

February 2018

The Second Growth

They didn't stop there. By now, they hired two new employees to the team: secretary Nathan and accountant Diane.

July 2018

The Love

After a year of working together, Daniel and Abby fell in love with each other and had a company-wide wedding.

Now and the Future

You

It is now up to you. You can help us with the future of our company and employees by allowing us to help you with the future of yours.
