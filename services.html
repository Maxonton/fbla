Overall Uses

The D∆EDALUS is a multifunctional robot that can change its software depending on the desired functionality, whether it be education or business. When used in an instructional setting, the D∆EDALUS employs an education software, and all the data collected by the D∆EDALUS robots are send and stored in a cloud network. This cloud helps the educational software amass data, learn from past experiences, and improve its assistance to teachers worldwide.

Public School

The D∆EDALUS is a sophisticated robot that can be used in various sectors of the education field. One of its important uses in the education sector from kindergarten to 12th grade is the ability for it to supplement curricula of a variety of courses. Since the D∆EDALUS has access to vast stockpiles of information though the cloud network, it can integrate this information to create personalized classes. This allows teachers to create a more specialized teaching environment designed to maximize student learning.

The D∆EDALUS also takes into account teacher preferences when designing curriculums, and uploads its own classroom experiences to the cloud network for other robots to access. Through this process, teachers benefit from a lighter workload and guidance in designing curriculums. Students too gain in having a curriculum that is designed to meet their needs, and one that is more conductive to learning. Overall, by helping teachers and students, the D∆EDALUS advances the education system as a whole, and allows the current generation of students to find success.

College

Due to the sophistication of the collegiate classes, the D∆EDALUS prioritizes different goals in the college environment. For example, it focuses more on hands-on and interactive learning to make complex topics easier to comprehend. The D∆EDALUS can even act as a teacher administrator, or TA, that not only directly aids the teacher, but also students. This TA can summarize professorial lectures and powerpoints for students, and provide assistance after class as well. For instance, it could review concepts that students are struggling with, or teach additional concepts to prepare students for future topics.

Not only does this make life easier for students who need assistance, but it also provides professors with greater control of the classroom settings, as students will fully understand the material being covered. Another important job for the D∆EDALUS as a TA would be to grade papers and tests, marking questions that many students have difficulty with and providing professors with additional material to help students understand the concept. Ultimately, the D∆EDALUS would allow college students to better study, learn, and excel.
